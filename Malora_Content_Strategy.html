Visual Design System

Send this section to your designer with every post brief. These rules are non-negotiable.

Design Rules — No Exceptions

  • No gradients — flat solid colors only
  • No emoji inside designed graphics — text icons if needed
  • Arabic font: Cairo or Noto Kufi Arabic — standard weight
  • English font: Inter or DM Sans — for names and numbers only
  • Sizes: 1:1 (Instagram feed) · 9:16 (Stories/Reels) · 1.91:1 (LinkedIn)
  • Logo: small corner placement — max 10% of canvas area
  • One CTA per design — never two
  • Arabic text: always right-aligned (RTL)
  • English text: always left-aligned (LTR)
